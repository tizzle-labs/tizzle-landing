FAQ

Common questions.

What wallets are supported?

Solana mobile wallets — Phantom and Solflare. You'll need one to register for events, receive tickets, and check in. Any wallet that supports the Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter will work.

How does ticket verification work?

Each ticket is a registration anchored on Solana. The venue scanner reads the QR, checks the on-chain registration state, and gets an instant valid or invalid — no server call, no guest list.

What is an organization?

Organizations are the official host entity on Tizzle. Create an org to get a public profile, event history, and analytics. You can then launch events under your organization's identity.

Can tickets be transferred or resold?

Because tickets are tied to your wallet's on-chain registration, the organizer controls transferability at event creation. Resale rules are set when the event is minted.

What is Solana devnet?

Devnet is Solana's test network — real blockchain infrastructure with no real money involved. Tizzle runs on devnet while we prepare for the mainnet launch.

Is this production-ready?

The app is live on Solana devnet with full functionality — browse events, register, buy tickets, and check in. We are actively working toward a mainnet launch.

Do I need a crypto wallet to join the waitlist?

No. The waitlist only needs your email. You will need a Solana mobile wallet when the app becomes available on your phone.
