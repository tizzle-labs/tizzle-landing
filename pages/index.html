FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need a crypto wallet to join the waitlist?

No. The waitlist is just an email. You only need a Solana wallet when the app launches.

How does stake-to-attend work?

When you register for an event, tokens are locked in an on-chain escrow vault. Attend and scan your QR — the contract marks you as checked-in and releases your stake after the unlock time. Don't show up and the organizer can configure partial or full forfeiture.

How does attendance verification work?

The venue scans the attendee's QR code. This triggers a check_in instruction on-chain, marking the registration as attended and unlocking the refund claim.

What tokens can I stake?

Native SOL, standard SPL tokens, and Token-2022 tokens are all supported. The organizer sets the accepted token when creating the event.

How are organizers charged?

Organizers pay a flat fee per attendee slot at event creation — not a percentage of revenue. Attendees are never charged a platform fee. Example: 100-capacity event × 0.0005 SOL = 0.05 SOL total.

Is this production-ready?

Not yet. This is currently running on Solana devnet — real infrastructure, no real money. We're validating the mechanism before moving to mainnet.
