FAQ

Common questions.

How do I install the demo app?

Download the APK from the Demo App section above and install it on your Android device. You'll need to enable "Install from unknown sources" in your settings. Check the Install Guide for a step-by-step walkthrough.

Do I need a crypto wallet to use Tizzle?

Yes. Tizzle uses Mobile Wallet Adapter (MWA) to connect your Solana wallet. For the demo, use any MWA-compatible wallet on Android — no real funds needed since it runs on devnet.

How does stake-to-attend work?

When you register for an event, SOL is locked in an on-chain escrow. Show up, get your QR scanned by the gatekeeper, and claim your full stake back after the unlock date. Don't show up — your stake goes to the organizer.

When is the refund available?

The unlock date is automatically set to 7 days after the event ends. Once that date passes and you've checked in, you can claim your full stake back directly from the app.

How are organizers charged?

Organizers pay a flat fee of 0.0005 SOL per attendee slot at event creation. Attendees are never charged a platform fee. Example: 100-capacity event = 0.05 SOL total.

Is this on mainnet?

Not yet. The demo runs on Solana devnet — real infrastructure, no real money. We're validating the full protocol before moving to mainnet.

Install Guide

1
Download the APK

Tap the Download APK button on this page. The file will be saved to your Android Downloads folder.

2
Allow unknown sources

Go to Settings → Apps → Special app access → Install unknown apps, then allow your browser or file manager to install apps.

3
Open & install the file

Open your Downloads folder, tap the tizzle.apk file, and follow the on-screen prompts to complete installation.

4
Connect your wallet

Open Tizzle and tap Connect Wallet. You'll need an MWA-compatible Solana wallet installed on your device (e.g. Phantom, Solflare).

5
You're on devnet

Tizzle runs on Solana devnet — no real funds are needed. Get free devnet SOL from a Solana faucet to start exploring events and staking.

Android 8.0 or higher required. If you run into issues, visit the Help page for detailed troubleshooting.
